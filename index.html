Ms Tina

Tina brings the newest technology from the United States, San Francisco 
Bay Area, region of the Silicon Valley, with her virtual reality classrooms. Holding a bachelor's degree in architecture design from the American Ivy League Cornell University In New York, Tina combines over 20-years of expertise in communications, the service industry and now education, to create and teach in these fully immersive, engaging and interactive VR environments. Tina is certified by the prestigious University of California Los Angeles (UCLA Extension) TESOL Program and offers her wealth of experiences living and working in the US, Europe and Asia to help learners from anywhere in the world to learn English as a second language.
